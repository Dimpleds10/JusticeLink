# JusticeLink India 🇮🇳⚖️  
### National Relief & Direct Benefit Transfer (DBT) Portal  
**SIH Problem ID: SIH25154**

---

## Problem Statement

**Modalities for implementation of Direct Benefit Transfer (DBT) under Centrally Sponsored Scheme for effective implementation of PCR Act and PoA Act**

The current relief disbursement process under:
- Protection of Civil Rights (PCR) Act, 1955  
- Scheduled Castes and Scheduled Tribes (Prevention of Atrocities) Act, 1989  

is largely manual, fragmented, and prone to delays.

JusticeLink India proposes a unified, AI-enabled national digital platform to ensure timely, transparent, and secure relief delivery.

---

## Project Overview

JusticeLink India – National Relief Portal is a centralized digital governance platform that connects:

- Victims
- NGOs
- District Officials
- State Authorities
- Central Ministry

It streamlines the complete workflow — from registration to verification to sanction to fund disbursement.

The system ensures:
- Transparency  
- Accountability  
- Privacy  
- Real-time tracking  
- Inclusive access
